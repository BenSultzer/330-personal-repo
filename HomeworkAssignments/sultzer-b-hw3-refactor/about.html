About Ultimate Audio Visualizer

This audio visualizer uses individual red particles of varying sizes, colors, and speeds to represent the frequency and waveform data of songs! A particle is larger, slower, and darker the lower the frequency, and smaller, faster, and lighter the higher the frequency. In the background, particle systems are used to represent frequencies as well. Higher frequencies have smaller particles with higher positions, and lower frequencies have bigger particles with lower positions (all are colored gray). Play around with the various options, including clicking in the canvas to exert a shockwave, and see what happens!
